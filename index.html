About Me

My name is Angel Sanchez, I am a Software Engineer based in Santa Ana, CA. I am a passionate learner, that immigrated to the states at the age of six. I have previously worked as an instructional provider for high school students helping them use their critical thinking skills in order to understand the material. What I have learned from this is the ability to find unique ways of approaching problems and finding out a variety of solutions. As a Software Engineer, I have further developed these skills to fill the needs of my clients.

In my free time, you can find me playing soccer, cooking, and watching my favorite marvel movie, spider-man. Feel free to check out my projects and contact me.
